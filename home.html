Our tours

If you're looking for a unique and unforgettable city-break, Norway is the perfect destination for you! With its stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant cities, Norway has something for everyone. Explore the charming streets of Bergen, with its colorful wooden houses and bustling fish market. Discover the historic treasures of Trondheim, with its Viking heritage and medieval architecture. Or experience the urban atmosphere of Oslo, with its world-class museums, trendy shops, and delicious food scene.

Experience the magic of summer in Norway with an unforgettable tour that will immerse you in the country's stunning natural beauty and vibrant cultural scene. Explore the pristine fjords, where sparkling waters and majestic cliffs create a breathtaking backdrop. Bask in the midnight sun, as the land of the Vikings comes alive with long days filled with outdoor adventures. Hike through lush forests, discovering hidden waterfalls and enchanting wildlife along the way. Whether you seek tranquility in nature or an exploration of the country's rich history and culture, a summer tour in Norway promises an unforgettable journey that will leave you with cherished memories for a lifetime.

Step into a winter wonderland and embark on a captivating tour through the magical landscapes of Norway. Experience the sheer beauty and enchantment of Norway's winters with a journey that will leave you spellbound. Witness the awe-inspiring Northern Lights dance across the Arctic sky, casting vibrant hues of green and purple. Embark on thrilling winter activities such as dog sledding, snowshoeing, and skiing through snowy forests and icy valleys. Delve into the rich cultural heritage of Norway by visiting traditional Sami villages and learning about their fascinating way of life. A winter tour in Norway promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you with cherished memories of a truly magical winter wonderland.

About us

Welcome to Norway, a country of breathtaking landscapes, stunning fjords, and vibrant cities! And what better way to explore all that Norway has to offer than with a trusted tourism agency? At our agency, we offer a wide range of tours and activities that cater to every type of traveler. Whether you're looking to hike through rugged mountains, kayak along crystal-clear waters, or simply take a leisurely stroll through charming villages, we've got you covered. Our knowledgeable guides are passionate about sharing their love for Norway with you, and will take you off the beaten path to discover hidden gems that you won't find in any guidebook. You'll also have the opportunity to meet locals and learn about their way of life, giving you a truly authentic Norwegian experience. One of the highlights of our tours is exploring Norway's world-famous fjords. We'll take you on a journey through some of the most breathtaking scenery in the world, from the stunning Geirangerfjord to the majestic Sognefjord. You'll have the chance to see towering mountains, cascading waterfalls, and even spot some wildlife along the way. But Norway isn't just about nature - our cities are also worth exploring. From the vibrant capital of Oslo to the charming coastal town of Bergen, there's something for everyone. You'll discover a rich history, vibrant culture, and delicious local cuisine as you explore Norway's cities with us. At our agency, we prioritize sustainability and responsible tourism. We strive to minimize our impact on the environment and support local communities, so you can feel good about the way you travel with us. So why wait?
